City of the Day: Herat

  • Historic significance: Center of art, learning, and architecture since the Timurid era.
  • Traditional crafts: Renowned for hand-woven carpets, miniature paintings, and calligraphy.
  • Literary heritage: A hub for Persian poetry and music; home to famous poets like Jami.
